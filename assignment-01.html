Travel Tech

Travel tech refers to the use of technology to enhance and streamline the travel experience for consumers and businesses in the travel industry. This encompasses a wide range of applications, tools, and innovations that make travel planning, booking, and experiencing more efficient and enjoyable. Here are some key areas and trends in travel tech:

  • Online Booking Platforms
  • Mobile Apps
  • Smart Airports and Airlines

Air Mobility

Air mobility refers to the transportation of people or goods by air, encompassing a wide range of technologies and innovations aimed at improving and expanding air transport. It includes traditional aviation but increasingly focuses on advanced air mobility (AAM) and urban air mobility (UAM).

Travel Agency

A travel agency is a business that assists individuals and groups in planning, booking, and managing their travel arrangements. Travel agencies can offer a wide range of services, from booking flights and accommodations to arranging tours and travel insurance. They cater to both leisure and business travelers, providing expertise and personalized services to enhance the travel experience.
